ENDING 1 By the time I opened my eyes again, I was already back in my own room. A heavy device was still strapped to my head, pressing painfully against my forehead. In an instant, I recalled everything. A few hours ago, after uploading the mental data from my brain to the spiritual network, I eagerly prepared for my first entry into my own mental world. However, at the same time, the spiritual network encountered an error, and a massive data leak occurred. Very coincidentially, I ended up entering the spiritual world of a user named Dave, whose data had been leaked. Fortunately, because I didn't answer the security questions correctly, the system's protective mechanisms forced me out of his world, allowing me to return. Now, seeing everything around me so real, so tangible, it is comforting. Anyway, I probably won't be trying this again anytime soon!
